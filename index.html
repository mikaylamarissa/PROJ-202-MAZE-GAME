Forklift Frenzy

Instructions

The goal of the game is to make your way through the maze as the forklift driver in training. The levels will start out easy and slowly get harder. Once you reach the end you will become a certified forklift operator.

To navigate through the maze you will use the arrow keys on your keyboard.

If you do not finish the maze in 1 minute you will have to start the level over.

Are you ready to become forklift certified?
